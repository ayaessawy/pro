1. The Illuminati

1.1 Who are the Illuminati?

The Illuminati are an ancient cabal who have accumulated vast wealth and power, which they wield to influence the world in ways that continually solidify their position as the dominant force on earth

Though the term 'Illuminati' is relatively recent, first appearing in relation to the founding of the Bavarian Illuminati in 1776, it is now used more broadly to denote a higher level organisation that has been influencing and manipulating the world for millennia.

The Illuminati’s ability to operate covertly and maintain secrecy makes it difficult to map and comprehend their activity with the same levels of certainty we might demand when approaching other more conventional subject matters. However, it is possible to come close by analysing patterns in global events, discerning their core motivations, and deciphering the signature strategies from the Illuminati playbook which they employ time and time again.

It is now more important than ever that we remain vigilant and informed in all matters regarding the Illuminati as we find ourselves in the midst of an incredibly unique time in human history as technologies which have the potential to drastically change our world have emerged. Technologies which the Illuminati will not hesitate to use against us.

1.2 What makes them so powerful?

The Illuminati have become immensely powerful, vastly outmatching the rest of society. They possess several tactical advantages that have enabled them to achieve this dominance:

Information advantage

They have advanced surveillance capabilities which allow them to monitor us at all times. They can see us but we cannot see them. They can steal our knowledge and technologies whilst we cannot steal theirs.

Covert strongholds

There are numerous hidden locations, most commonly underground, which allow the Illuminati to operate away from public scrutiny, and beyond the reach of any national or international laws. Here they establish infrastructure, conduct R&D, store resources, etc.

Alternative moral framework

The Illuminati do not believe in traditional concepts of right and wrong. This allows them to engage in activities that we would find morally objectionable, such as conducting large-scale human experimentation with technologies that carry the risk of harm and death. This freedom enables them to advance scientifically much faster than we do, as well as gain a host of other strategic benefits.

Wealth generation

They possess the ability to generate wealth through both the manipulation of our financial markets and the malicious infiltration of the computer systems that manage our financial systems. Wealth, as we all know, is power. It can be used to influence politics, the media, corporations, and individuals, as well as for the creation of infrastructure and the development of technologies.

Technological advantage

achieve superiority in every field, most notably those that grant strategic advantages such as computing, AI, robotics, nanotechnology, surveillance, genetics, psychology, and physical/biological weaponry.

Psychological warfare

They are masters of psychological manipulation and employ numerous techniques to prevent us from rising up against them:

They use ideologies and fear mongering to keep us divided and working against each other instead of uniting into the larger structures which might stand a chance of overcoming their power.

They use confidence tricks by supporting and helping individual groups with the aim of gaining their trust and fooling them into believing the Illuminati are on ‘their side’. A trust which is always eventually betrayed.

They muzzle society by discouraging the use of 'plain' English, instead encouraging indirect forms of communication; forms which are effective at concealing information but cripple our ability to have meaningful discussion about complex issues. Forms which also make it difficult to organise mass action.

1.3 The higher purpose that guides all of their actions.

The Illuminati do not subscribe to conventional moral frameworks. They do not believe in concepts such as ‘good’ and ‘evil’. They operate under an alternative system built around the service of a higher purpose that guides all of their actions, an eternal higher purpose that supersedes all else, rendering us expendable in its pursuit. It can most simply be described as:

To seek continuous development and advancement to ensure that the human race (or another form of consciousness) survives as far into the future as possible.

The Illuminati believe that our only real purpose, as it is with all living creatures, is to survive. What sets us apart from the rest of the animal kingdom is that as intelligent beings we know that survival requires more than merely following natural instincts. Long-term survival requires us to advance human knowledge and develop the technologies needed to enable us to survive future cataclysmic events such as a climate collapse, the death of our sun, the end of our galaxy, and perhaps even the eventual demise of the universe itself.

There are many who share in this belief and work to do their part to advance human progress. What separates the Illuminati is the extreme lengths to which they will go to serve the higher purpose, not just seeking the expansion of human knowledge, but also pursuing radicaladvancements of the human race itself.
